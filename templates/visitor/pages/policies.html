Placement Policies

Guidelines and procedures for the placement process

Eligibility Criteria

  • Academic Performance: Students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 6.0 (or equivalent percentage) or as per company criteria throughout their academic program.
  • Backlogs: Students with more than 2 active backlogs are not eligible for placement activities.
  • Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory in all subjects.
  • Disciplinary Record: Students must have a clean disciplinary record with no pending disciplinary actions.
  • Documentation: All required documents including resume, academic transcripts, and certificates must be submitted on time.

Registration Process

  1. Online Registration: Students must register on the placement portal with accurate personal and academic information.
  2. Document Verification: Submit all required documents for verification by the placement cell.
  3. Profile Completion: Complete your profile including skills, projects, internships, and achievements.
  4. Resume Upload: Upload a well-formatted resume following the placement cell's template guidelines.
  5. Approval: Wait for approval from the placement cell before applying to companies.

Application Guidelines

  • Students can apply to multiple companies but must follow the application deadlines strictly.
  • Once shortlisted, students must attend all selection rounds (aptitude test, technical interview, HR interview) as scheduled.
  • Withdrawal from the selection process after accepting an offer is not permitted without valid reasons and prior approval.
  • Students must maintain professional conduct throughout the placement process.
  • Any misrepresentation of information will lead to disqualification from the placement process.
  • Submission of false or misleading information will result in immediate disqualification from the placement process.

Offer Acceptance Policy

  • Students receiving job offers must respond within the specified deadline (typically 48-72 hours).
  • Accepting an offer is a binding commitment. Students cannot participate in further placement activities after accepting an offer.
  • If a student receives multiple offers, they must choose one and inform the placement cell immediately.
  • Rejection of an offer after acceptance may result in disciplinary action and barring from further placement activities.

Dress Code & Conduct

Dress Code:
  • Formal attire is mandatory for all placement activities (interviews, group discussions, etc.)
  • Men: Formal shirt, trousers, tie, and formal shoes
  • Women: Formal shirt/blouse with formal trousers/skirt or saree/salwar kameez
Code of Conduct:
  • Maintain punctuality for all placement activities
  • Respect company representatives and placement cell coordinators
  • No use of mobile phones during interviews or tests
  • Maintain confidentiality of placement-related information
Important Notes
  • All placement-related communications will be sent through the official placement portal and registered email.
  • Students must regularly check the portal for updates and announcements.
  • The placement cell reserves the right to modify these policies as per institutional requirements.
  • Submission of fake documents or incorrect information will lead to strict disciplinary action.
  • For any queries or clarifications, contact the placement cell office during working hours.
